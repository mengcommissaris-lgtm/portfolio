M1.2 Design Research

Mie and you against the Sunday blues

U&S | T&R | C&A | M,D,C

A design research on how to help employees who experience the Sunday blue, a negative mood shift on Sunday afternoons or evenings, often due to anticipation of upcoming workweeks or regrets about previous weekends, impacting their overall well-being and work. Following the iterative design cycles, a personalized support character was developed that helped users create meaningful weekends and reflect on their weekends. Laying a foundation for better understanding the Sunday blues and potential ways of offering guidance.
