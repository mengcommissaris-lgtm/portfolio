Design for Social Innovation

WeCare cafe

U&S | B&E

A project focused on product sustainability and social innovation. Through an ethnographic study on repurposing old fabrics into a menstrual blanket, an opportunity was identified to share this knowledge with women in Eindhoven through a community café. The concept provided a safe and accessible space for learning about menstruation, exchanging experiences, and creating personalized reusable period pads, thereby reducing reliance on single-use products and decreasing plastic waste.
