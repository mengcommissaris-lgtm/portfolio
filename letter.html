Constructive Design Research

Letter Sorter

U&S | T&R | M,D,C

A design research on how to help international students feel less overwhelmed by receiving Dutch governmental letters. Through interviews and user-testing, a smart letter sorter was developed that provides international students with explaining what type of governmental letters they received and help them sort them based on importance, lowering the perceived stress of receiving letters.
